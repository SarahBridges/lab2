The International Union for the Conservation of Nature (IUCN) assigns each animal on its endangered list a status ranging from least concern to extinct.

  • LC = Least Concern
  • NT= Near Threatened
  • VU=Vulnerable
  • EN=Endangered
  • CR=Critically Endangered
  • EW=Extinct in the Wild
  • EX=Extinct

Many species of seahorses are vulnerable. For a species to be vulnerable, less than 10,000 mature individuals exist in the wild.
